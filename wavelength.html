How to Play:

  • One player is the Psychic - they can see the target zone
  • The Psychic gives a clue for where a concept falls on the spectrum
  • Example: For "Hot vs Cold" with target on the far hot side, clue might be "The Sun"
  • Team discusses and moves the dial to guess the target location
  • Press Lock In Guess to reveal how close you were
  • Scoring: Green center (5pts), Yellow sides (1pt), Miss (0pts)

Goal: Get as close to the center of the green target zone as possible!
